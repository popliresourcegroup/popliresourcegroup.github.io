Popli Resource Group LTD 
 Popli Resource Group Ltd. is an established trading firm in Japan. We specialize mainly in the export of Used/New Cranes and Piling Machinery from Japan such as, Crawler Cranes, Rough Terrain Cranes, Mobile Cranes, Piling Rigs, Vibro Hammers, Hydraulic Hammers, Excavators, Bulldozers, Rotatory Augers, etc. We also offer Used Ships, Tug-boats, NC Lathe Machines and various other Plant & Machinery.
Machinery used and maintained in Japan is highly sought after due to its well maintained quality and durable remaining life. We would be pleased to meet your requirement of any kind of used machinery from Japan.
